For Spaces

Code-based ambient visuals for calm architectural spaces — lobbies, lounges, hotels, clinics, saunas, cafes, and residential common areas.

I create quiet moving images that do not demand attention, but slowly change the atmosphere of a room. The work is minimal, meditative, and designed to stay in the background for a long time.

Available for screen installations, looping visuals, custom commissions, and small spatial experiments.

Works

A collection of code-based visual experiments. Some became spatial works, while others remain studies in motion, color, interaction, and atmosphere.

Immiscible

2026 / HTML Canvas

A particle-based fluid simulation (SPH) of two immiscible liquids — water and oil — in a different jewel-toned color pair on every load. Surface tension pulls each liquid together while they refuse to mix; drag to stir them into swirling marble, then watch them slowly separate again. Tilt your device to change the direction of gravity. Built with plain HTML and Canvas.

A Branching Study

2026 / HTML Canvas

A branching fractal study that moves through selected compositions of fine, dividing lines. Each cut introduces a newly generated structure, while color shifts through complementary relationships between the branches and the background. Drag to change the color atmosphere and double-tap to enter fullscreen. Built with plain HTML and Canvas.
